About

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Engineering at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. My research focuses on neuromorphic computing, spiking neural networks, and memristive in-memory computing systems.

My work bridges device-level modeling, mixed-signal circuit design, and hardware-aware learning algorithms to enable energy-efficient, biologically inspired AI systems for vision, continual learning, and edge intelligence.
